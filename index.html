Aligned Pedagogy

NCERT GPT draws inspiration from Harvard's CS50 AI tools, integrating pedagogical techniques that promote independent learning and critical thinking:

  • 1:1 Learning Support: Like Harvard's approach, NCERT GPT acts as a personal tutor, accessible anytime for tailored assistance.
  • Guided Learning: We emphasize understanding and exploration, helping students think critically rather than providing direct answers.
  • Pedagogical Guardrails: Our system ensures ethical use of AI, mirroring Harvard's "guardrailed" approach to meaningful learning.

These methodologies ensure NCERT GPT is not just a tool, but a trusted educational companion for all students, whether you're working through Class 6 textbooks or preparing for the UPSC exam.
